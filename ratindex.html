R
The Rat is the first zodiac sign in the Oriental zodiac cycle.
According to the Oriental zodiac story, in the competition held by the
Jade Emperor to decide the zodiac animals, the quick-witted rat
asked the diligent ox to take him on a ride to cross the river and jumped
down before the ox crossed the finish line. Thus the rat won the race and
became the first of the zodiac animals.


Personality: quick-witted, resourceful, versatile, kind.
